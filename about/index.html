About Truckee Snowbotics

Truckee Snowbotics is FTC Team #32587 based in Truckee, California — in the heart of the Tahoe-Truckee region. We're a student-led robotics team at Tahoe-Truckee High School competing in the FIRST Tech Challenge, where we design, build, and program robots while developing real-world engineering and teamwork skills. We compete in the Northern Nevada FTC region and are passionate about inspiring the next generation of STEM leaders in our mountain community.
