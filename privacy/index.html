Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Overview

Truckee Snowbotics (FTC Team #32587) operates the website at snowbotics.org. This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

We collect information only when you voluntarily submit it to us:

  • Contact form — When you send us a message, your name, email address, and message content are processed by Formspree and forwarded to our team email. We use this information solely to respond to your inquiry.
  • Sponsorship inquiries — If you contact us about sponsorship, we may retain your contact information to follow up.

We do not use cookies, tracking scripts, or analytics services on this site.

Third-Party Services

  • GitHub Pages — This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may log standard server access data (IP address, browser type, page requested). See GitHub's Privacy Statement.
  • Formspree — Our contact form is powered by Formspree. See Formspree's Privacy Policy.
  • Hack Club Bank (HCB) — Donations are processed through HCB. See Hack Club's Privacy Policy.

Data Retention

We do not store personal data on our own servers. Information submitted via our contact form is retained only as long as needed to respond to your inquiry.

Children's Privacy

Our website is operated by and for students. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors without parental consent. Contact us if you have concerns.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at contact@snowbotics.org.
